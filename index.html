FAQ/Guides

First things First

  • Where to get Freebies?

    • Now that you're in the game, you'll find yourself in Northern Flarine Go find [Novelist] Low Tier NPC this is where you get your first items to help you level up and farm for better gears

      NOTE: Freebies costs 1 penya so you might want to go outside Flarine town and kill an Aibatt first

  • How to Teleport?

    • If you've been playing flyff before you're familiar with Blinkwings and the hassle to buy them or forgetting to buy one and also it takes space in your inventory.. well not in Quantum

      You can just press 'F' to open the teleport menu and teleport, as many times as you'd like (as long as you have enough penya, its really cheap) cool huh? :D

  • What effect does the Couple Buffs give?
    • Power of Love: Increased Attack +250% and Increased HP +250%
    • Blessing of Love: All stats +1000
    • Miracle of Love: Lucky box +1
  • What effect does the raised pets give?
    • [Tiger] = STR
    • [Lion] = STA
    • [Rabbit] = DEX
    • [Fox] = INT
    • [Dragon] = Increased Attack %
    • [Griffin] = Increased MP %
    • [Unicorn] = Increased HP %
